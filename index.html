The Real Reason

Why Spain?

This isn't a career move dressed up as a life choice. Spain has been the destination long before the job search began.

It started with FC Barcelona — watching Xavi, Iniesta, and Messi play a kind of football that felt like art. The tiki-taka, the pressing, the identity. It wasn't just a team, it was a philosophy. And that philosophy — build something beautiful through intelligence and precision — is honestly how I try to approach data too.

Then came Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara — a film that painted Spain as a place where people go to truly live. The tomato fights of La Tomatina, the running of the bulls in Pamplona, the flamenco in Granada, the sunsets over the Mediterranean. It lodged itself in my head and never left.

I captained my college football team. I perform on stage. I write screenplays. I am someone who needs colour, energy, and passion in their surroundings — and Spain has all of that in abundance. The culture, the language I am actively learning, the warmth of the people. It fits.

Spain is not the backup plan. Spain is the only plan.
